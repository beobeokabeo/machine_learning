# Computing on data

> How to do computations on data

1. Load data into Octave

```MATLAB
>>A = [1 2; 3 4; 5 6]
A =

   1   2
   3   4
   5   6

>>B = [11 12; 13 14; 15 16]
B =

   11   12
   13   14
   15   16

>>C = [1 1; 2 2]
C =

   1   1
   2   2

>>A*C
ans =

    5    5
   11   11
   17   17

>>A .* B % the period denotes element-wise operation
ans =

   11   24
   39   56
   75   96

>>A .^ 2
ans =

    1    4
    9   16
   25   36

>>v = [1;2;3]
v =

   1
   2
   3

>>1 ./ v
ans =

   1.00000
   0.50000
   0.33333

>>1 ./ A
ans =

   1.00000   0.50000
   0.33333   0.25000
   0.20000   0.16667

>>log(v)
ans =

   0.00000
   0.69315
   1.09861

>>exp(v)
ans =

    2.7183
    7.3891
   20.0855

>>v
v =

   1
   2
   3

>>abs(v)
ans =

   1
   2
   3

>>abs([-1; 2; -3])
ans =

   1
   2
   3

>>-v % -1*v
ans =

  -1
  -2
  -3

>>v + ones(length(v),1)
ans =

   2
   3
   4

>>length(v)
ans =  3
>>ones(3,1)
ans =

   1
   1
   1

>>v + ones(3,1)
ans =

   2
   3
   4

>>v+1
ans =

   2
   3
   4


>>A
A =

   1   2
   3   4
   5   6

>>A'
ans =

   1   3   5
   2   4   6

>>(A')'
ans =

   1   2
   3   4
   5   6

>>a = [1 15 2 0.5]
a =

    1.00000   15.00000    2.00000    0.50000

>>val = max(a)
val =  15
>>[val, ind] = max(a)
val =  15
ind =  2
```
